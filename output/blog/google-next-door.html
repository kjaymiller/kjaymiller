Why does Google want to make a new Nextdoor?

> "In practice, it looks a lot like a Google version of Nextdoor, a similar local social media network you can use to talk to people near you and broadcast your thoughts to friends and colleagues around the world." I agree with this idea but I think that Google can do a lot with that information to make their _Knowledge of Everything_ even more powerful. Imaging combining the images and overall rating system of yelp with the non-aggressive thoughts behind Neighborly. You have a pretty cool profile created for businesses when you search for things.
